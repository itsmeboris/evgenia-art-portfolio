Meet Evgenia Portnov

Welcome to my artistic journey. I'm Evgenia Portnov, an artist who discovered the transformative power of art during one of life's most challenging chapters.

My work blends realism, abstraction, and mystic elements, primarily focusing on the beauty of flowers, the charm of towns, and the freedom of birds. Through acrylic colors, I've found a voice to express emotions and perspectives that words alone cannot capture.

Each piece I create is infused with a sense of positivity and hope—emotions that have guided my own path through difficult times. I believe that art has the power not only to beautify spaces but also to heal hearts and inspire resilience.

I invite you to explore my collections and perhaps find a piece that speaks to your own journey.

My Artistic Journey

My journey as an artist began in 2024 during a profoundly challenging period in my life. When my husband became an amputee following an accident, our world was suddenly transformed. During those difficult days of adjustment and healing, I found myself drawn to canvas and paint as a way to process my emotions and find moments of peace.

What started as a form of personal therapy quickly evolved into a passionate pursuit. With each brushstroke, I discovered not only a new skill but also a renewed sense of purpose. Art became my sanctuary—a place where challenges could be transformed into beauty and where difficult emotions found positive expression.

I gravitated toward three main themes in my work:

  • Flowers - symbols of resilience, beauty, and the cyclical nature of life
  • Towns - representing community, connection, and the structures that support us
  • Birds - embodying freedom, perspective, and the ability to rise above challenges

As my technical skills developed, so did my artistic voice. I found myself naturally blending elements of realism with abstract and mystic influences, creating pieces that invite viewers to see beyond the literal and connect with the emotional essence of each subject.

Today, I create art with the intention of spreading positivity and reminding others that even in life's most challenging moments, there is beauty to be found and created. Each painting is not just an artwork but a testament to resilience and the transformative power of creative expression.

"I paint not just what I see, but what I feel—the resilience of nature, the warmth of community, and the freedom of spirit that helps us transcend life's challenges. Through my art, I hope to share the peace and purpose I've found, inviting others to discover their own sources of strength and beauty."
