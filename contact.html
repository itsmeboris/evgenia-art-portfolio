Frequently Asked Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, I ship artwork worldwide. International shipping costs will be calculated at checkout based on your location and the size of the artwork.

Can I commission a custom piece?

Absolutely! I welcome custom commissions. Please contact me with details about what you have in mind, including preferred dimensions, theme, and any specific elements you'd like incorporated.

How are the paintings packaged for shipping?

All artwork is carefully packaged to ensure safe delivery. Paintings are wrapped in acid-free tissue paper, protected with bubble wrap and foam board, and shipped in sturdy boxes with additional protective materials.

What is your return policy?

If you're not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return it within 14 days of delivery. The artwork must be in its original condition and packaging. Please contact me before initiating a return.
